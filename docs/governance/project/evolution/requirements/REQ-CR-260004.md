# REQ-CR-260004: MVP Deletion Safety Constraints for Graph Editing

- Class: CR
- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-04-17
- Source decision: D5 (Deletion and safety)

## Requirement

MVP deletion behavior shall use hard-delete with safety constraints: atom deletion requires confirmation, atom/bond deletion provides an undo window, and bulk-delete workflows remain out of MVP scope.

## Rationale

Safety constraints reduce destructive-error risk while preserving lightweight editing behavior in MVP.

## Scope Notes

- In scope for MVP: confirmation for atom delete and undo affordance for atom/bond deletion.
- Out of scope for this requirement: soft-delete lifecycle and bulk-delete operations.

## Verification

- Behavior checks confirm atom delete prompts explicit confirmation.
- Behavior checks confirm undo is available after atom and bond deletion.
- Scope checks confirm bulk-delete is not exposed in MVP UI.
